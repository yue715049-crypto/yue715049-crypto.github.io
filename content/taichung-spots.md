Planning a trip to Taichung? Here are the top 5 attractions you shouldn't miss in 2025!

<div class="spot-item">
    <h2 class="spot-title">1. Gaomei Wetlands (高美濕地)</h2>
    <div class="spot-info">
        <strong>Address:</strong> Qingshui District, Taichung City<br>
        <strong>Highlights:</strong> Sunset, Wind Turbines, Boardwalk
    </div>
    <p>Known for its stunning sunsets and rich biodiversity, Gaomei Wetlands is a must-visit. Walk along the boardwalk and enjoy the sea breeze.</p>
    <img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1596522354195-e8448e9b0d5e?q=80&w=800&auto=format&fit=crop" alt="Gaomei Wetlands">
</div>

<div class="spot-item">
    <h2 class="spot-title">2. Rainbow Village (彩虹眷村)</h2>
    <div class="spot-info">
        <strong>Address:</strong> Nantun District, Taichung City<br>
        <strong>Highlights:</strong> Colorful Murals, Art, Photography
    </div>
    <p>A vibrant village painted by "Rainbow Grandpa". Every corner is a photo opportunity.</p>
    <img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1571474004502-c18476e81579?q=80&w=800&auto=format&fit=crop" alt="Rainbow Village">
</div>

<div class="spot-item">
    <h2 class="spot-title">3. National Taichung Theater (臺中國家歌劇院)</h2>
    <div class="spot-info">
        <strong>Address:</strong> Xitun District, Taichung City<br>
        <strong>Highlights:</strong> Architecture, Arts, Rooftop Garden
    </div>
    <p>Designed by Toyo Ito, this opera house is an architectural marvel with its curved walls and sound cave concept.</p>
    <img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1595840208734-754599553650?q=80&w=800&auto=format&fit=crop" alt="National Taichung Theater">
</div>

<div class="spot-item">
    <h2 class="spot-title">4. Miyahara (宮原眼科)</h2>
    <div class="spot-info">
        <strong>Address:</strong> Central District, Taichung City<br>
        <strong>Highlights:</strong> Ice Cream, Souvenirs, Harry Potter Vibes
    </div>
    <p>Formerly an eye clinic, now a famous dessert shop with a Harry Potter-esque interior.</p>
    <img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1552423316-6f391763738e?q=80&w=800&auto=format&fit=crop" alt="Miyahara">
</div>

<div class="spot-item">
    <h2 class="spot-title">5. Shenji New Village (審計新村)</h2>
    <div class="spot-info">
        <strong>Address:</strong> West District, Taichung City<br>
        <strong>Highlights:</strong> Market, Crafts, Coffee
    </div>
    <p>A creative cluster converted from old dormitories, featuring artisan shops and weekend markets.</p>
    <img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1558258695-5e638e5b0265?q=80&w=800&auto=format&fit=crop" alt="Shenji New Village">
</div>
