【2025 Taichung Attractions】Top 5 Must-Visit Spots in Taichung!

By Tingyue Lai 五 05 12月 2025

Advertisement

Planning a trip to Taichung? Here are the top 5 attractions you shouldn't miss in 2025!

1. Gaomei Wetlands (高美濕地)

Address: Qingshui District, Taichung City
Highlights: Sunset, Wind Turbines, Boardwalk

Known for its stunning sunsets and rich biodiversity, Gaomei Wetlands is a must-visit. Walk along the boardwalk and enjoy the sea breeze.

2. Rainbow Village (彩虹眷村)

Address: Nantun District, Taichung City
Highlights: Colorful Murals, Art, Photography

A vibrant village painted by "Rainbow Grandpa". Every corner is a photo opportunity.

3. National Taichung Theater (臺中國家歌劇院)

Address: Xitun District, Taichung City
Highlights: Architecture, Arts, Rooftop Garden

Designed by Toyo Ito, this opera house is an architectural marvel with its curved walls and sound cave concept.

4. Miyahara (宮原眼科)

Address: Central District, Taichung City
Highlights: Ice Cream, Souvenirs, Harry Potter Vibes

Formerly an eye clinic, now a famous dessert shop with a Harry Potter-esque interior.

5. Shenji New Village (審計新村)

Address: West District, Taichung City
Highlights: Market, Crafts, Coffee

A creative cluster converted from old dormitories, featuring artisan shops and weekend markets.
